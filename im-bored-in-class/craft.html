    About Eaglercraft

    Eaglercraft brings the thrill of sandbox survival and creativity to players who love open-world freedom. Inspired by Minecraft, this game lets you craft, explore, and fight for survival while building incredible structures in an expansive world. Whether you prefer to go solo or team up with others, Eaglercraft offers multiple ways to enjoy adventure-packed gameplay!

    Game Modes – Choose Your Experience

    Survival Mode – Gather, Craft, and Stay Alive

    • Collect resources from the wild—gather wood, stone, and food to survive.

    • Craft essential tools and weapons to protect yourself from dangers.

    • Monitor your avatar’s food and water needs, ensuring you stay strong in harsh conditions.

    Hardcore Mode – The Ultimate Test

    • You have only one life—if you die, the game ends!

    • Survival is tougher—every move needs careful planning.

    • Watch out for hidden dangers in the environment, as you have no second chances.

    Creative Mode – Build Without Limits

    • Access endless blocks and materials for limitless construction.

    • No hunger, no enemies—just pure imagination and design!

    • Find the perfect spot and craft your dream world from scratch.

    Multiplayer – Team Up & Conquer

    • Play on public servers with players worldwide.

    • Join forces or challenge others in survival and creative worlds.

    • Create your own server with the "Add Server" button and design your own adventure!
